About us

What We Do

We fund operations that prevent plastic waste from ending up in our oceans, landfills, and foodchains. We take plastic pledges and provide ways to offset footprints through credible and verifiable post-consumer plastic removal from our environment.

Our operations currently take place across the Philippines and we are working on international expansion.
